About

Serbian hardcore/metalcore band from Zrenjanin. Band was formed in 2002. at first they were called From The Deep and in 2004. they've recorded a demo 'We Are Coming', and soon after that new band members came, and name was changed to Except One. After some band members left in 2007. name was changed yet again to All Except One.

Band members:


Branislav Čarkić aka Baki - Guitar/Vocal
Stevan Mišić aka Stiv - Vocal
Franja Fuks aka Fule - Drums
Vanja Rakić aka Vanjak - Guitar
Nemanja Đatkov aka Đale - Bass
